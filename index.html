About Me:

Hello! I'm Isha Younas, a dedicated and enthusiastic software developer with a strong passion for creating engaging, user-friendly web applications. I enjoy working with technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always looking to expand my knowledge and skill set.

Beyond coding, I have a keen interest in UI/UX design, enjoy reading tech articles, and value a good walk to recharge. I’m currently seeking internship opportunities where I can contribute, continue learning, and grow as a developer in a collaborative environment.
